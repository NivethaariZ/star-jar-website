3–5 business days across India. We pack with love so your jar arrives safely.

Absolutely! Enter your personal wish in the order form and we'll hand-write it on a scroll inside the jar.

Is every star hand-folded?

Yes! Every single paper star is folded by hand with care and love. No machines involved.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, and Bank Transfer. Payment details shared on WhatsApp after order confirmation.

Can I customize the star colors?

Yes! Mention your preferred colors in the wish field and we'll match them as best as we can.
